rebranding an it company.














This project is my rebrand to IT People Innovation company.



Background



It-people innovation is a portuguese based company committed in providing costumized IT services for medium and large companies. The company develops medium and large projects in a variety of technologies including web, mobile and business intelligence/big data. They act upon three major areas: consulting, outsourcing and augmented reality.




Exploring concepts





The idea that supports the whole logo design process rests in the fact that i want to convey a subliminal meaning. The triangle/pyramidal shape represent the company as a whole, along with the 3 main areas of action, with a strong base. The electronic synapses represent the IT area of action, while the right side of the pyramid represents the augmented reality, playing with a tri-dimensional effect.
