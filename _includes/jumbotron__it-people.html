constructing my personal brand.














My personal logo is the combination of my initials N+G fused togheter, in a clean and simplistic approach.



Exploring



The ideation process started with the basic notion that both of my initials should be incorporated into one another. By making it collapsable in one direction the hope was to achieve greater foldability so it reads as a single N.




Colours scheme





I kept the same mindset of simplicity for the colours, chosing three colours as base and generating a custom palette for the overal branding.

The final design implemented in various materials.
