The app that ensures the party never stop.










The aim of this project was to solve a problem of our current days: everyone is more than often looking and interacting with their mobile devices, even at parties. In a way, this app aims to help people to focus in the present moment they are.



Exploring

How would it work? The dj would add a qr code for the event, which would grant the user access to the event database, and allow to rate, discover and share music. This would help dj's to better understand his market niche and deliver playlists suited for each one. This would also increase popularity and awareness to their work.
