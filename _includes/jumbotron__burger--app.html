A burger app to solve a problem.














I created this app for a local burger place, that has grown really fast and did not have an online presence other than it's facebook page. As a regular costumer and a problem solver, i took it upon myself to find a solution.



Exploring



After conducting research on competitive analysis to better understand the market context, i managed to gather user feedback from friends, who are also clients of the company to better develop a user flow.

The new logo process.



The next step was building a high fidelity wireframing with balsamiq mockups to achieve a more polished and finalized look.




Interaction and animation





I kept the same mindset of simplicity for the colours, chosing three colours as base and generating a custom palette for the overall branding.
